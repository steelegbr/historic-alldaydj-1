News / Hourly Settings

On this screen are the settings for the news / top of the hour.

The first textbox is the file that will play a set number of seconds before the top of the hour.

The seconds text box sets the length (in seconds) of the above item.

The last text box is the news bed or file. Even if left blank, the players will hold for 3 mins.

To enable / disable news playout, select the checkbox.

AllDay DJ

Recommendations

Below is a list of remmoendation to make your AllDay DJ experience better.

Use AllDay DJ On A Dedicated MachineMachines used for other tasks such as internet browsing will over time reduce the resourses available to AllDay DJ.
Use A ScreensaverAdmittedly this uses RAM but it is worthwhile compared to the cost of a new monitor due do screen burn.
Use A High Performance A Mahine If PossibleSimplified, AllDay DJ is playing several sound files at once. The more resourses available, the better.
Backup Your DataIf you network your AllDay DJ Machine(s), you can have central music storage on another machine and update the database for all your machines simultaniously. This allows you to make backups of the data. The files you need to backup are logs.mdb, record_collection.mdb, news.dat, station.dat, rotation.dat & soundcard.dat These store your settings and music database.

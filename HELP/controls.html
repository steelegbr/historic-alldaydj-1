AllDay DJ

Controls

These are the main player controls.

Clicking on the Play button will play the first file on the playlist and will continue until the playlist is complete.

Clicking on the Automate button will play any items on the playlist, then enter automatic rotation.

Clicking on the Stop button will stop any items playing out and will cancel automatic rotation.



These are the playlist controls

Add, Remove and Clear add to, remove from and clear the playlist.

Next item stops the current item and starts the next item on the playlist.

Break after toggles whether the playout will stop after the current item.

The up and down arrow keys are used to re-order the playlist. To move and item up or down, highlight the item by clicking on the artist name and select the up or down key.

The Save Playlist and Load Playlist buttons are used to load and create pre-defined playlists.
NOTE: AllDay DJ PlayList files (.addpl) are database specific and therefore not transferable between databases.



The top two timers show you the time until the news jingle starts and current time left on the playlist.

Note: Dependant on the admin settings, the time until news timer may not appear.

The rest of the timers and text boxes show you the Title, Artist and position in the current item.

Note: The countdown timer will turn green during the intro countdown.



The instant players are loaded with the sound effects / jingles by selecting load. This will show a standard loading screen.

The players can be started by selecting the appropriate button or by selecting one of the Function keys (e.g. F1) on the keyboard.

Repeating this action will restart the player from the beginning of the item.
